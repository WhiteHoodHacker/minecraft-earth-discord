1. Be cool, kind, and civil. Treat all members with respect and express your thoughts in a constructive manner. 2. Use English only. Communicate in English, but be considerate of all languages. 3. Use an appropriate name and avatar. Avoid special characters, emoji, obscenities, and impersonation. 4. Do not spam. Avoid excessive messages, images, formatting, emoji, commands, and @mentions. 5. Do not @mention or direct message Minecraft Earth staff. 6. No self-promotion or advertisements. This includes unsolicited references and links to other social media, servers, communities, and services in chat or direct messages. 7. No personal information. Protect your privacy and the privacy of others. 8. No harassment, abuse, or bullying. We have zero-tolerance for harming others. 9. No racist, sexist, anti-LGBTQ+, or otherwise offensive content. We have zero-tolerance for hate speech. 10. No political or religious topics. These complex subjects result in controversial and offensive posts. 11. No piracy, sexual, NSFW, or otherwise suspicious content. We do not condone illegal or suspicious discussions and activity. 12. Do not promote or encourage the use of exploits and unintended game behavior. If you come across any exploits or bugs, please let the staff know or report it to the bug tracker. 13. Rules are subject to common sense. These rules are not comprehensive and use of loopholes to violate the spirit of these rules is subject to enforcement. 14. Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines apply. You must be at least 13 years old to use Discord, and abide by all other terms and guidelines. Direct message or ping an available Moderator if you experience or witness inappropriate content or behavior. You may mention the @Moderator or @Administrator role for activity in channels that requires immediate moderation. (edited)
