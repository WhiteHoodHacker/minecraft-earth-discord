Build a bright and expressive build using any form of glass in any combination of colors (you can use other blocks too)! Challenge dates: July 14 – July 27 @ 6 PM PT
ProfessorValko July 14, 2020 at 8:11 PM (UTC-07:00)
It’s time for a Glass Build Challenge! In the interest of full transparency, glass is great! Stained glass is even better! So, put some sand in the furnace and grab some dye. Build a bright and expressive build using any form of glass in any combination of colors (you can use other blocks too)! Will you craft an architectural wonder, shining masterpiece, or something else that we’d never see coming (even though we can totally see through glass)? You have the full rainbow of glass blocks at your fingertips! Challenge dates: July 14 – July 27 @ 6 PM PT Challenge Guidelines Your creation must be built entirely by you and in Minecraft Earth. Your build must be a brand new creation, crafted just for this challenge. Build your imagination! Challenge theme is open to interpretation and buildplate size won’t be a factor in judging. Post your images or video submission and buildplate share link in #glass-wonders-challenge by the end date. Feel free to ask a Moderator to pin it! Submissions may be used in social media posts. Have fun!
