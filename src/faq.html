Gameplay
What is Minecraft Earth? Minecraft Earth is an augmented reality mobile game that brings the Minecraft world into our universe! Build creations with friends and place them in the real world at life-size, discover new mobs to use in your builds, and put your survival skills to the test as you battle Minecraft mobs in real life! Can I use skins or customize my character? Yes. You can use skins that you own from Minecraft Marketplace skin packs or customize your appearance using the in-game Character Creator. Minecraft Earth does not support importing external skins. Can I build and play with friends? Yes! You can invite nearby friends to build with you in your buildplate or play an Adventure together! Place your buildplate on a surface or Adventure on the ground, and tap ‚ÄúInvite Friend‚Äù. Your friends can tap "Join Friend" from the Buildplate or Adventure menu to scan your QR code. You and your friends need to be in the same location to play together. (edited)
What are Adventures? Adventures are the survival part of Minecraft Earth. They are slices of a Minecraft world in life-size on the ground. Activate an Adventure Crystal to place an Adventure on your in-game map and tap on it to play! Dig down and explore‚Äîyou might find mobs, valuable resources, hidden treasures, and other surprises! What are Adventure Crystals? Adventure Crystals are in-game items that you can use to activate a private Adventure that only you and friends who you invite to play with you can see! You can find Adventure Crystals in tappables, daily challenges, and in Adventure Chests at the end of each Adventure! What are Rubies? Rubies are a virtual currency that you can earn from tappables or purchase in the Store. Each time you collect from a tappable, you have a chance to find Rubies! You can use Rubies to buy things in the Store, such as buildplates and Boosts. What are Boosts? Boosts are in-game items that you can use to enhance your abilities for a short amount of time. You can use Rubies to buy Boosts in the Store, or by purchasing a Minecraft Earth Boost Mini figure and activating it in-game with an NFC-capable device. Learn more about Boosts and Boost Mini figures: https://help.minecraft.net/hc/en-us/articles/360039922812 (edited)
